Summary

"Inception" follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within people's subconscious during dreams. Cobb is offered a chance at redemption if he can successfully plant an idea into someone’s mind—a process known as inception. As the team ventures deeper into layered dreams, reality begins to unravel.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with a talented ensemble including Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Cillian Murphy.
